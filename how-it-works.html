How It Works

Our streamlined process connects you with off-market land opportunities that match your exact specifications.

1

Submit Your Buy Box Criteria

Everything starts with understanding exactly what you're looking for. Through our simple online form, you provide us with your specific requirements:

  • Location: Specific cities, counties, or regions in Tennessee or Las Vegas
  • Property Size: Minimum and maximum acreage
  • Budget Range: Your investment parameters
  • Zoning Requirements: Residential, commercial, or specific zoning needs
  • Special Criteria: Utilities, access, topography, or other must-haves

The more specific you are, the better we can match opportunities to your needs. Don't worry—you can update your criteria anytime as your needs evolve.

2

We Source Off-Market Properties

This is where our expertise and network come into play. We actively source land opportunities through:

  • Direct Seller Outreach: We contact property owners before they list on the open market
  • Network Connections: Relationships with local landowners, estate attorneys, and professionals
  • Market Research: Continuous monitoring of target areas for emerging opportunities
  • Off-Market Channels: Access to properties that never hit the MLS

We're not just passively waiting for listings—we're actively hunting for properties that match your buy box.

3

Property Vetting & Qualification

Not every property we find makes it to your inbox. We perform initial due diligence to ensure opportunities are legitimate and viable:

  • Title Verification: Confirm clear title and ownership
  • Zoning Check: Verify current zoning matches your requirements
  • Basic Assessment: Review access, utilities availability, and major characteristics
  • Pricing Analysis: Ensure asking prices align with market value and your budget

We filter out problematic properties so you only see genuine opportunities worth your consideration.

4

Receive Exclusive Opportunities

When we find a property that matches your buy box, we send you a detailed property package including:

  • Property Details: Size, location, zoning, and key features
  • Pricing Information: Asking price and any relevant pricing context
  • Photos/Maps: Visual overview of the property and location
  • Due Diligence Summary: What we've verified and what requires further investigation
  • Contact Timeline: How quickly you need to respond if interested

You'll receive these opportunities directly via email, giving you first look before anyone else.

5

Review & Decision

You're in control of the decision. When you receive an opportunity:

  • Review at Your Pace: Take time to evaluate the opportunity (within any stated deadlines)
  • Request More Information: We can provide additional details or arrange property visits
  • Perform Your Due Diligence: Conduct whatever additional research you need
  • Pass or Proceed: Decide if you want to move forward or pass on the opportunity

There's no pressure and no obligation. If a property doesn't fit, simply pass and we'll keep looking.

6

Facilitate the Transaction

When you decide to move forward on a property:

  • Direct Connection: We facilitate introductions between you and the seller
  • Transaction Support: We help coordinate the deal structure and timeline
  • Closing Assistance: We remain available throughout the closing process
  • Transparent Terms: All fees and terms are clearly outlined upfront

Our goal is to make the transaction as smooth as possible while ensuring both parties reach a successful close.

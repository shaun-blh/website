Submit Your Buy Box

Tell us exactly what you're looking for and we'll start sourcing targeted off-market opportunities.

What Information Do We Need?

The more specific you are about your requirements, the better we can match opportunities to your needs. Please provide as much detail as possible about:

  • Your target markets (specific cities, counties, or areas in Tennessee or Las Vegas)
  • Property size requirements (minimum and maximum acreage)
  • Budget range (your investment parameters)
  • Zoning preferences (residential, commercial, agricultural, etc.)
  • Special requirements (utilities, road access, topography, etc.)
  • Your contact information so we can reach you with opportunities

What Happens Next?

After you submit your buy box criteria, we'll review your requirements and begin actively sourcing properties that match your specifications. You can expect to hear from us within 24-48 hours to confirm your criteria and discuss any questions. From there, we'll start sending you targeted opportunities as we find them.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a cost to submit my buy box?

No, submitting your buy box criteria is completely free. We only earn a fee if you decide to purchase a property we've sourced for you, and all fees are transparent and disclosed upfront.

Can I update my criteria later?

Absolutely! Your needs may change over time, and you can update your buy box criteria at any time by contacting us directly or resubmitting the form with updated information.

How quickly will I start receiving opportunities?

Timeline varies based on your specific criteria and current market availability. Some builders receive opportunities within days, while others with very specific requirements may take a few weeks. We're constantly sourcing, so the more flexible your criteria, the more frequently you'll see opportunities.

Am I obligated to purchase any properties you send me?

Not at all. There's no obligation whatsoever. We send you opportunities that match your criteria, and you decide which ones (if any) you want to pursue. You're in complete control of your purchasing decisions.

Have Questions Before Submitting?

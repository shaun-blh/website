Submit Your Buy Box

Take 5-10 minutes to tell us exactly what you're looking for

What Information Do We Need?

The more specific you are about your requirements, the better we can match opportunities to your needs. Please provide as much detail as possible about:

  • Your target markets — Specific cities, counties, or areas in Tennessee or Las Vegas
  • Property size requirements — Minimum and maximum acreage you're considering
  • Budget range — Your investment parameters and price per acre targets
  • Zoning preferences — Residential, commercial, agricultural, or specific zoning needs
  • Timeline — When you're looking to acquire (immediately, next 6 months, etc.)
  • Special requirements — Utilities, road access, topography, environmental factors
  • Your contact information — So we can reach you with opportunities

What to expect: We'll review your criteria within 24 hours, our team will start sourcing matching properties, and you'll receive updates when we find opportunities.

Confidentiality: Your information is kept confidential and will only be used to find properties matching your criteria.

What Happens Next?

After you submit your buy box criteria, we'll review your requirements and begin actively sourcing properties that match your specifications. You can expect to hear from us within 24-48 hours to confirm your criteria and discuss any questions. From there, we'll start sending you targeted opportunities as we find them—no spam, just properties that truly match what you're looking for.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a buy box?

Your buy box is your ideal property criteria—location, size, zoning, budget, and any other requirements. It helps us find exactly what you need rather than showing you properties that don't fit.

Is there a cost to submit my buy box?

No, submitting your buy box criteria is completely free. We make money on the wholesale transaction when you purchase a property through us. Our fee is built into the final price you see—no hidden costs.

What markets do you serve?

We currently focus on Tennessee (statewide) and Las Vegas, Nevada with deep expertise in both markets. We understand local zoning, development trends, and what builders need to succeed in these areas.

How quickly can I see opportunities?

Timing varies based on market conditions and how specific your criteria are. Some builders receive opportunities within days, while others with very specific requirements may take a few weeks. Once we have your buy box, we actively source properties and will keep you updated throughout the process.

Can I update my criteria later?

Absolutely! Your needs may change over time, and you can update your buy box criteria at any time by contacting us directly or resubmitting the form with updated information. We want to stay aligned with your current needs.

Am I obligated to purchase any properties you send me?

Not at all. There's no obligation whatsoever. We send you opportunities that match your criteria, and you decide which ones (if any) you want to pursue. You're in complete control of your purchasing decisions.

What type of properties do you source?

We source both residential and commercial land suitable for development. This includes single-family lots, multi-family parcels, commercial sites, and mixed-use opportunities.

Do you only work with builders?

While builders are our primary focus, we also work with real estate investors looking for development opportunities. If you're acquiring land for development purposes, we can help.

How is this different from working with a real estate agent?

Traditional agents show you what's currently listed. We source properties specifically for your criteria, including off-market opportunities. Our buy-box approach means you only see properties that truly match your needs—no wasted time on irrelevant listings.

What due diligence do you perform?

We conduct preliminary due diligence on zoning, utilities, and development potential. However, we always recommend buyers conduct their own thorough due diligence before purchasing.

Can I submit multiple buy boxes?

Yes! If you're working on different types of projects or looking in multiple areas with different criteria, you can submit separate buy boxes for each. This helps us better target opportunities for your various needs.

Have Questions Before Submitting?

We're here to help. Reach out and we'll discuss how we can best serve your land acquisition needs.
